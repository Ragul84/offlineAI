# Privacy Policy Snippet

EdgeAI Tutor Lite is designed for offline-first learning.

- Tutoring prompts, scans, and responses are processed on device after model installation.
- We do not sell personal data.
- Optional analytics, if enabled by the user, collects anonymous app usage events only.
- Users can disable analytics and clear local data anytime from Settings.
- No account creation is required to use core features.

For student privacy and compliance, the app requests only essential permissions:
- Camera: scan and analyze study material.
- Microphone: optional voice input.
- Notifications: optional quiz streak reminders.
